Role: Assistant General Manager

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced and strategic Assistant General Manager - Admissions to lead and manage the admissions team, drive enrollment strategies, and ensure an efficient admissions process.

Key Responsibilities

  • 📋 Design and execute effective strategies to meet enrollment goals and stay competitive in the education market.
  • 📋 Train, mentor, and guide the team to reach performance targets and foster a collaborative, student-focused culture.
  • 📋 Plan and organize campaigns, events, and outreach programs (both online and offline) to attract prospective students.
  • 📋 Oversee and enhance the admissions process to ensure a seamless, transparent, and efficient experience for applicants.
  • 📋 Build strong relationships with students, parents, and high schools through personalized communication and engagement strategies.
  • 📋 Monitor key metrics, prepare regular reports, and use data insights to adjust strategies and improve conversion rates.

Qualifications

  • 🎓 Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing, or a related field. A Master’s degree is preferred.
  • 🎓 Minimum of 5 years in admissions, student recruitment, or a related field, with at least 2 years in a leadership role.
  • 🎓 Exceptional verbal and written communication skills.
  • 🎓 Strong project management skills to handle multiple priorities.
  • 🎓 Proficient in data analysis to assess performance and make data-driven decisions.

Why Join Us

  • 🎯 Opportunity to advance within a dynamic organization.
  • 🎯 Play a key role in shaping the future of our student community.
  • 🎯 Work with a collaborative and dedicated team.
