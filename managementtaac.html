Wholeness in Harmony
Hands, Heart, and Heads in Motion

  • "Hands" symbolize the power of action, encouraging students to apply practical skills and knowledge.
  • "Heart" reflects our dedication to nurturing compassion, integrity, and emotional intelligence, fostering a caring and empathetic community.
  • "Heads" represent intellectual growth, driving critical thinking and academic excellence.

At TAAC, our motto represents the harmony of three core pillars: Hands, Heart, and Heads. Together, these elements guide our students to become well-rounded individuals, equipped to face challenges with skill, passion, and wisdom.
